Let me sing some praises.

This website is dedicated to the glorious season known as Summer, and why it is so great.

Why is Summer so great?

  • Summer is the optimum time for visiting a pool, whether to take a refreshing dip, tan, catch some Zzzs, or read a book.
  • Another perfect Summer activity is to visit a beach, whether you are fortunate enough to live close to one or travel to one.
  • Summer is prime time for prime ribs, and anything else you can cook up on a barbecue. There are so many holidays in the season to use as an excuse to chow down!
  • The long days of Summer are the perfect time for catching up with friends over a cool drink!
"I adore everything about Summer. I live for the heat. Give me 90 degree days for all eternity. This website is just so great because it reminds me of everything I love about the season."
- A Happy Visitor
